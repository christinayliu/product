What is it?
Product is a product.
It's like Lorum Ipsum, but for this website template. Product is a mobile-friendly website template designed to have an intuitive feel and simple and smooth UX/UI. It is designed for startups or other companies who are trying to showcase a single (you guessed it) product.
Open up
your mind to the possibilities. Product can be anything*
* Product is just a cardboard box.
Applications
Computers
Product was made with computers. Using conservation of matter and energy, it follows that computers can be made with Product. It's simple logic: if a Computer is a Product, then a Product is a Computer.
Robots
Product can be used to ship robots, trip robots, flip robots, tip robots, grip robots, dip robots, zip robots...
Cats
Place a cat in Product and it will tell you if the cat is dead or alive. Or use product as a litterbox if you want
